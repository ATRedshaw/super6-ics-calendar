Stay up-to-date with the latest Super 6 deadlines. Add this calendar to your preferred calendar app.

How to Add the Calendar to Your App

Use the instructions below to add the Super 6 calendar to your favorite calendar app:

Google Calendar
Outlook Calendar
Apple Calendar

Google Calendar

To add the Super 6 calendar to Google Calendar:

  1. Copy the calendar URL: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ATRedshaw/super6-ics-calendar/main/super6-calendar.ics
  2. Go to Google Calendar.
  3. On the left panel, find "Other calendars" and click the "+" sign.
  4. Select "From URL" and paste the copied URL.
  5. Click "Add Calendar" to subscribe to the Super 6 calendar.

Outlook Calendar

To add the Super 6 calendar to Outlook:

  1. Copy the calendar URL: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ATRedshaw/super6-ics-calendar/main/super6-calendar.ics
  2. Go to Outlook Calendar.
  3. On the left panel, click "Add calendar" and select "Subscribe from Web".
  4. Paste the copied URL into the "Link to the calendar" field.
  5. Click "Import" to subscribe to the Super 6 calendar.

Apple Calendar

To add the Super 6 calendar to Apple Calendar:

  1. Copy the calendar URL: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ATRedshaw/super6-ics-calendar/main/super6-calendar.ics
  2. Open the Apple Calendar app on your Mac.
  3. Click "File" in the menu and choose "New Calendar Subscription".
  4. Paste the copied URL into the "Calendar URL" field.
  5. Click "Subscribe" to add the Super 6 calendar.
